Ishwar Gaikar

"How Ishwar Gayakar earned Rs 2.8 crore in one Month"

Ishwar Gayakar, along with his wife, hail from Pune's Junnar taluka. After earning a reported Rs 2.8 crore already, they have set an ambitious target of reaching Rs 3.5 crore with their current stock of approximately 4,000 crates of tomatoes.

Banswad Mahipal Reddy

"How Banswad Mahipal Reddy earned Rs 1.8 crore in one Month"

A tomato farmer earned 1.84 crores in one month by selling tomatoes. It is nowhere in our Telangana. Food vendor Banswad Mahipal Reddy of Mohammad Nagar in Kaudipalli mandal of Medaku district became a millionaire by selling tomatoes.
